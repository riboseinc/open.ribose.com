= Ribose named Best New Collaboration Service and Most Innovative Company in Asia, Australia and New Zealand by 2015 International Business Awards

*Company wins two Gold Stevie® Awards in world's premier business awards program*

Hong Kong -- 13 August 2015 -- Ribose was named winner of two Gold Stevie®
Awards in the "`Best New Product or Service of the Year -- Software
Collaboration/Social Networking Solution`" and "`Most Innovative Company
of the Year in Asia, Australia and New Zealand`" categories in The 12^th^
Annual International Business Awards today.

The International Business Awards are the world's premier business
awards program. All individuals and organizations worldwide -- public and
private, for-profit and non-profit, large and small -- are eligible to
submit nominations. The 2015 IBAs received entries from more than 60
nations and territories. More than 3,700 nominations from organizations
of all sizes and in virtually every industry were submitted this year
for consideration in a wide range of categories.

The Ribose cloud collaboration platform democratizes collaboration
technology, previously only affordable to large enterprises and
government organizations, by putting world-class security and technology
into the hands of all users. Capturing the attention of the technology
and business worlds, Ribose has been featured in Fortune and TechTarget.

Accessible anywhere, offering enterprise-level service dependability,
and complying with international governance standards -- the Ribose
platform gives users peace of mind to focus on their tasks at hand.
Advocating a 'highest-bar' approach to cloud security, Ribose provides a
higher level of security for all users by meeting the most stringent
specified requirements worldwide.

According to Ribose founder Ronald Tse, "`Ribose is delighted to have won
not only one, but two Gold Stevie Awards in the hotly contested 'Best
New Product or Service of the Year' and 'Most Innovative Company of the
Year in Asia, Australia and New Zealand' categories at this year's
International Business Awards. These awards stand as testament to our
mission of providing world-class collaboration capability to make
working together easy and fun -- while protecting users' data with the
highest, internationally certified levels of security.`"

He continued, "`We would like to thank the organizers for putting this
year's awards together, and are humbled by the expert judges'
recognition of our work -- especially considering the impressive list of
nominees from around the globe. On behalf of our team at Ribose, we
congratulate all of this year's award winners and nominees. It is an
honor to be included amongst such distinguished company.`"

Stevie Award winners were determined by the average scores of more than
200 executives worldwide who participated in the judging process from
May through early August.

IBA Judges commended the Ribose platform for democratizing secure
collaboration, by providing a "`technically robust offering`" with
"`collaboration tools in a secured setting`", allowing an unprecedented
flexibility to use the "`open platform`" in both "`professional working and
fun`" settings through a "very easy to use and attractive interface
design" -- as winning factors in naming Ribose's platform the Best New
Product or Service of the Year in the Software
Collaboration/Social-Network Solution category. Ribose's security focus
was best summarized by a judge who noted: "`Most existing cloud
collaboration software simply does not offer the necessary security
levels to protect mission-critical, commercially sensitive projects.`"

In naming Ribose the Most Innovative Company of the Year in Asia,
Australia and New Zealand, IBA Judges lauded the company's "`great
commitment and great team, deploying the technology to the benefit of
both the company and the community,`" "`professional management`" and
"`excellent performance`", as well as its "`impressive accomplishment in
achieving multiple certifications`", providing users with "`all the
benefits of an enterprise-class collaboration platform, with none of the
associated infrastructure and costs.`"

"`We congratulate Ribose on winning Two Gold Stevie Awards in the 12th
Annual International Business Awards. This year, the Stevie Awards
received over 3,700 entries lauding stories of success and achievement
around the world. Out of all these world-class entries, the fact that
our judges selected Ribose for two Gold Stevie Awards stands as
testament to the company's innovation and reputation for excellence,`"
said Michael Gallagher, president and founder of the Stevie Awards.

Details about The International Business Awards and the lists of Stevie
Award winners are available at
http://www.stevieawards.com/IBA[www.StevieAwards.com/IBA].

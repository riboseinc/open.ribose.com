= Ribose achieves Asia's first ISO 14001:2015 and ISO 9001:2015 certifications!

We're the first (outside of UK) to achieve the new ISO 14001:2015 and
ISO 9001:2015 certifications! Goes to show how committed we are to
excellent governance. Woo hoo!

1. Both certifications are awarded by BSI and accredited by ANAB, the
only Accreditation Body that has approved CBs as of now.

2. Our 14001 certification is the 2nd accreditated certification in
BSI, and of worldwide! (which is the only accredited CB currently)

3. Our 9001 certification is among the first 4 accreditated
certifications from BSI (3 are from UK)!

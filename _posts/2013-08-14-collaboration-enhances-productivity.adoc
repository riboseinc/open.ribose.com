= Collaboration enhances productivity

Collaboration enhances productivity. There is so much to be gained from
collaboration — it can open your mind to new ideas, different opinions,
and a fresh perspective. The process of collaboration creates room for
dialogue that can affirm your existing vision, challenge it, and
ultimately strengthen it. For any occasion in your life, find
opportunities to collaborate with others, even if it is just bouncing
ideas back and forth with a friend.
